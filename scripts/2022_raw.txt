Q.9
Subject: General Aptitude
Topic: Analytical Aptitude
Type: MCQ
Year: 2022
Difficulty: Hard
Marks: 2
Negative Marks: 0.66
Question Text:
In a class of five students P, Q, R, S and T, only one student is known to have copied in the exam. The disciplinary committee recorded the following statements:
Statement of P: R has copied in the exam.
Statement of Q: S has copied in the exam.
Statement of R: P did not copy in the exam.
Statement of S: Only one of us is telling the truth.
Statement of T: R is telling the truth.
The investigating team had authentic information that S never lies. Based on the information given above, the person who has copied in the exam is:
Options:
A) R
B) P
C) Q
D) T
Correct Answer: B
Explanation:
Since S never lies, S's statement "Only one of us is telling the truth" is TRUE. This means exactly one person among P, Q, R, S, T is telling the truth. S itself is telling the truth, so S is that one person — meaning all others (P, Q, R, T) must be lying.
Since R is lying, R's statement "P did not copy" is FALSE, meaning P did copy.
Since T is lying, T's statement "R is telling the truth" is FALSE — consistent since R is lying.
Since P is lying, P's statement "R has copied" is FALSE.
Since Q is lying, Q's statement "S has copied" is FALSE.
Therefore, P has copied in the exam.

The Taylor series expansion around x = 0 of the function f(x) = (x + 1) / (eˣ + 1) truncated to first two terms is:
Options:
A) 1/2 + (1/4)x
B) 1/2 + (1/2)x
C) 1/2 + x
D) 1/2 + 2x
Correct Answer: A
Explanation:
At x = 0: f(0) = (0 + 1)/(1 + 1) = 1/2. This is the first term.
For the second term, compute f'(x) using the quotient rule:

At x = 0: f'(0) = [(1 + 1) − (1)(1)] / (1 + 1)² = [2 − 1] / 4 = 1/4.
Therefore f(x) ≈ 1/2 + (1/4)x.

According to Sieverts' law, the equilibrium solubility of N₂ (gas) in molten steel is proportional to ________. Given: Equilibrium partial pressure of N₂ (gas) is p(N₂).

B) √p(N₂)
C) 1/p(N₂)
D) p(N₂)²
Correct Answer: B
Explanation:
